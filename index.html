Stray Streets offers solutions.

TNR programs, or Trap-Neuter-Return, offer a humane alternative to the influx of cat euthanizations across the country. Stray Streets hopes to serve as a starting point for public education regarding outdoor cat issues, and provide valuable tips and resources for how you can become involved in your own community.
